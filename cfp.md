# RubyConf 2026 — Call for Papers

Source: https://sessionize.com/rubyconf-2026/

## Key Dates

- **CFP Opens:** February 10, 2026
- **Submission Deadline:** March 15, 2026
- **Review Period:** March 16-23, 2026
- **Results Announced:** March 30 - April 6, 2026
- **Conference:** July 14-16, 2026

## Location

Red Rock Casino Resort & Spa, Las Vegas, Nevada, United States

## Talk Formats

- **Standard Sessions:** Up to 30 minutes with optional Q&A
- **Workshops:** Two-hour blocks on Wednesday only (July 15), typically hands-on with attendees working on their own applications

## Themes

1. **Living with the Robots** — AI-assisted programming applications and productivity gains with Ruby
2. **Beautiful Ruby** — Software design principles, modularity, and maintainable code practices
3. **Weird Ruby** — Creative, experimental, or unconventional Ruby projects

## Submission Requirements

- Proposals must include full name, institutional affiliation (if applicable), and a brief bio (100 words maximum)
- Review process is anonymous — reviewers only see title, description, and notes

## Speaker Benefits

- Full conference ticket
- Up to $500 travel reimbursement (subject to fund availability)
- VIP speaker reception
- Optional speaker coaching and liaison support
